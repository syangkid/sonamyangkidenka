About Me

Hello, I'm Sonam

Passionate Designer & Artist from Bhutan

I am a creative designer based in Thimphu, Bhutan, with a deep passion for visual storytelling and brand identity. My work bridges the rich visual heritage of Bhutanese culture with contemporary design principles.

Currently studying Data Science and Data Analytics, I bring an academic understanding of aesthetics alongside hands-on experience in graphic design, branding, and digital illustration. Every project I take on is an opportunity to create something meaningful and memorable.

When I'm not designing, I'm exploring the mountains of Bhutan, finding inspiration in the traditional art, architecture, and colours that surround me.
